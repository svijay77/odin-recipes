Milkshake

Description

This is a yummy milkshake. You can add some ice if you like it really cold. This stuff is really good. Would work well with some vanilla protein.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Blend milk, avocado, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.
